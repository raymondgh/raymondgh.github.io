Your simple daily budget

Introducing Budgie

Budgie is the only app with a daily allowance that goes up every day instead of down. Your spendable budget is front and center. Anything left unspent at the end of the day rolls over into tomorrow's balance. It's simple. It's fun.

Stash that cash!

When you've got a little extra at the end of the day, save it!

At any time, contribute to one of your financial savings goals and see the effects in real time. Budgie gives you total control over your finances, whether your money's in your wallet or in your bank.

It feels great to accomplish goals, and Budgie helps with just that.
